Elena Ruiz

"The Weight of Suitcases"

Elena Ruiz is a journalist and essayist whose work explores the liminal spaces between cultures, languages, and identities. Born in Mexico City and now based in Brooklyn, her writing has appeared in The Atlantic, Guernica, and Catapult.

Immigration

Excerpt from the Essay

I left everything I knew at thirty-two. Not because I was brave, but because staying had become more terrifying than the unknown.

The essay continues with Elena's journey across borders—both geographical and emotional—exploring what it means to carry home in your heart when you can no longer carry it in your hands. A meditation on loss, adaptation, and the unexpected places we find belonging.

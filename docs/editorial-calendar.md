## Phase 2: Author Recruitment (Weeks 5-10)

### Week 5-6: First Wave Outreach
- [ ] Send outreach to top 10 author targets
- [ ] Follow up after 7 days if no response
- [ ] Track responses in simple spreadsheet

### Week 7-8: Second Wave
- [ ] Send outreach to next 10 targets
- [ ] Follow up with first wave
- [ ] Begin onboarding confirmed authors (send agreement, guidelines)

### Week 9-10: Finalize Contributors
- [ ] Goal: 5-8 confirmed authors minimum
- [ ] If under target, expand outreach or adjust scope
- [ ] All confirmed authors receive welcome email + materials

**Checkpoint**: Minimum 5 confirmed contributors to proceed

## Phase 4: Editorial (Weeks 17-20)

### Week 17-18: First Read & Feedback
- [ ] Complete first read of all submissions
- [ ] Send developmental feedback within 2 weeks
- [ ] Note any essays needing significant revision

### Week 19-20: Revisions
- [ ] Authors submit revisions
- [ ] Final editorial review
- [ ] Copyedit all essays
- [ ] Finalize essay order/sequence

**Deadline**: All essays finalized by end of Week 20

### Week 23: Review

- [ ] Author review of their individual pages
- [ ] Final corrections

### Week 24: Pre-Launch
- [ ] Generate final PDF
- [ ] Build web pages for all essays
- [ ] Test all links and downloads
- [ ] Prepare launch assets (social graphics, email templates)

- **Day 1**: Send email to subscriber list
- **Day 1**: Publish web version

- [ ] Continue author spotlights

## Contingency Plans

### If we don't hit 5 contributors by Week 10:
- Extend outreach by 2 weeks
- Consider launching with fewer essays (minimum 3)
- Push launch date if needed

### If essays are late:
- Built-in buffer: 1 week between deadline and editorial start
- Can accept late submissions up to Week 17
- After Week 17, defer to Edition Two

### If design takes longer:
- Prioritize web version (can launch without PDF)
- PDF can follow 1-2 weeks post-launch

---

## Tracking

Use simple spreadsheet with:
- Author name
- Outreach date
- Response (Y/N/Pending)
- Agreement signed (Y/N)
- Submission received (Y/N)
- Editorial status (Draft/Feedback sent/Revised/Final)
- Bio & photo received (Y/N)

## 1. The Work

The Contributor agrees to provide one original personal essay ("the Work") for publication in Vox Mosaic Edition [Number]. The Work will:

- Be between 1,500 and 3,000 words
- Align with the edition's theme of transformation/becoming
- Be the Contributor's original creation
- Not have been previously published in substantially the same form (unless disclosed and agreed upon)

## 2. Grant of Rights

The Contributor grants Vox Mosaic a **non-exclusive license** to:

- Publish the Work in the digital edition on voxmosaic.com
- Include the Work in downloadable PDF versions of the edition
- Use excerpts (up to 300 words) for promotional purposes
- Feature the Contributor's name, bio, and likeness in connection with the Work

The Contributor **retains all other rights** to the Work, including the right to:

- Republish elsewhere after the 30-day exclusivity window
- Adapt, expand, or include in future collections
- Pursue traditional or self-publishing opportunities

## 3. Exclusivity Window

The Contributor agrees not to publish the Work elsewhere for **30 days** following the official edition launch date. After this window, the Contributor may republish freely, with a courtesy mention of Vox Mosaic appreciated but not required.

## 4. Editorial Process

Vox Mosaic may suggest edits for clarity, consistency, and style. All substantive changes require the Contributor's approval. The Contributor will have the opportunity to review the final version before publication.

## 5. Contributor Materials

Along with the Work, the Contributor will provide:

- A short bio (50-100 words)
- A high-resolution headshot
- Links to website, newsletter, or social media (optional)

These materials will be used for the Contributor's author profile and promotional purposes.

## 6. Compensation

For Edition One, there is no upfront payment. The Contributor receives:

- Cross-promotion to all contributors' audiences
- A permanent author profile on voxmosaic.com
- Featured placement in the designed digital publication
- Shareable promotional assets

If Vox Mosaic generates revenue through this edition (premium versions, print, sponsorship), the Contributor will receive a pro-rata share as determined by Vox Mosaic.

## 7. Warranties

The Contributor represents that:

- The Work is original and does not infringe on any third party's rights
- The Work does not contain defamatory content
- The Contributor has the authority to grant these rights

## 8. Withdrawal

If the Contributor must withdraw the Work before publication, they agree to notify Vox Mosaic as soon as possible, and no later than 14 days before the scheduled launch date.

## 9. Term

This agreement is effective upon signing and continues for as long as the Work remains published on Vox Mosaic platforms. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice, at which point the Work will be removed from Vox Mosaic.
